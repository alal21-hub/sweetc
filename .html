Our Story

Founded in 2010, Sweet Crumb started with a simple mission: to bring the warmth of homemade baking back to the neighborhood. We use only organic flour, farm-fresh eggs, and real butter to create pastries that taste like home.

Every morning at 4 AM, our ovens are lit, filling the streets with the scent of cinnamon and yeast.
